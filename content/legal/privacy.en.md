## 4. Retention and Destruction of Personal Information

In principle, the Company destroys Members' personal information immediately after the purpose for which it was retained has been achieved.

The Company may retain information provided at the time of membership registration from the time of registration until the date the withdrawal request is received. However, to prepare for unwanted membership withdrawals due to identity theft, personal information shall be retained for 7 days after a withdrawal request. In accordance with internal policies to prevent disputes arising from fraudulent use of the Service, records of fraudulent use — that is, personal information of Members who have engaged in conduct in violation of the Terms of Service and Company policies — shall be retained for 1 year.

The procedures, deadlines, and methods for destruction of personal information are as follows.

**Destruction Procedure**

Personal information whose purpose of collection and use has been achieved is transferred to a separate database, safely stored in compliance with internal regulations and applicable laws, and destroyed without delay when the prescribed period has ended. Personal information transferred to a separate database shall not be used for any purpose other than that to which each individual consented, unless required by law.

**Destruction Method**

Information in electronic file format is permanently deleted using technical methods that prevent recovery. Personal information printed on paper is destroyed by shredding or incineration.
