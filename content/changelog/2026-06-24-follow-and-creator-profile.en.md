## Follow Creators & SNS Links

You can now follow creators whose recipes you love, and find their Instagram, YouTube, and blog links right on their profile.

### How It Works

1. Go to a creator's profile page
2. Tap **Follow** to start following
3. Tap the "Followers · Following" count to see the list

### Tips

- Add your Instagram, YouTube, and blog links from the profile edit page
- Revisit a creator's profile to catch their newest recipes
- Tap any name in the followers/following list to jump straight to their profile
